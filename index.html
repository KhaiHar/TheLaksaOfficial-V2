About Us

The Laksa Official was established in 2019, located at Kontena Park, Unit 7. Originally, we were a home based business selling laksa curry. The laksa broth is home-made, cooked with love and passion. With a lot of support and prosperity, we officially open our restaurant at Kontena Park, Unit 7.

Our Laksa provides a Premium Curry Laksa cooked with high quality and best ingredients! Served with 3 types of options of your choices, "Chicken Laksa", "Prawn Laksa" and our top seller, "The Special Laksa" which are the combination of chicken and prawn.
